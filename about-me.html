About Me

My name is Jean-Pierre Reiners. As French as the name sounds and otherwise is; I am actually of South American decent. My background has enstilled in me an unwavering sense of optimism in my day to day life. A quality I will often leverage to push through any and all obstacles in the way of accomplishing my goals. My works are reflective of my current interest; meaning I will often work in realms I have little to no experience in as a means of learning. I have real world experience, both personal projects and as part of larger productions, in photography, video production, and public speaking.

I have ACA Certifications in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. Likewise I am experienced in Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Lightroom Classic, and Adobe Audition.

I have spoken on behalf and in representation of several programs in my coumminty. This has included conference keynotes, informative presentations, and board meetings.
